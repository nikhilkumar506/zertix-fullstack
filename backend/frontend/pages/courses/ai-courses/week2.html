⚙️ Practical Understanding

Machine Learning systems learn patterns from data. The more quality data they receive, the better they perform. Real-world ML systems are used in recommendation engines, predictive analytics, and automation.

📊 Types of Learning

  • Supervised Learning (labeled data)
  • Unsupervised Learning (pattern discovery)
  • Reinforcement Learning (trial & reward)
