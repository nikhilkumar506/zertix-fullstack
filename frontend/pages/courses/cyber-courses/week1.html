📘 What is Cyber Security?

Cyber Security refers to protecting systems, networks, and data from digital attacks such as hacking, malware, and phishing.

🌍 Real-World Case Study

The WannaCry ransomware attack (2017) infected over 200,000 computers globally, locking data and demanding Bitcoin payments.

🧠 Key Learnings

  • Importance of cybersecurity
  • Types of cyber threats
  • Impact of attacks
  • Security basics

🛠 Practical Insight

Use strong passwords, enable 2FA, and avoid suspicious links.
