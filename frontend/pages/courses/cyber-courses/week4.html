📘 What is Encryption?

Encryption is the process of converting readable data (plaintext) into an unreadable format (ciphertext) to protect it from unauthorized access.

🔑 Types of Encryption

  • Symmetric Encryption: Same key for encryption & decryption (AES)
  • Asymmetric Encryption: Public + Private key (RSA)

🌍 Real-World Example

When you access a banking website, HTTPS encrypts your data. Even if hackers intercept the data, they cannot read it without the decryption key.

🌐 What is HTTPS?

HTTPS uses SSL/TLS encryption to secure communication between your browser and the server.

  • Encrypts data
  • Prevents interception
  • Ensures secure login

🛡️ Data Protection Techniques

  • Encryption
  • Hashing (password protection)
  • Access control
  • Data backup

🧠 Practical Tips

  • Always use HTTPS websites
  • Never share OTPs or passwords
  • Use password managers
  • Enable encryption on devices
