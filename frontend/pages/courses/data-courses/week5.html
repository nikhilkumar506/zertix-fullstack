📘 What is Data Visualization?

Data visualization is the graphical representation of data using charts, graphs, and dashboards. It helps businesses quickly understand patterns, trends, and insights.

⚠️ Why Visualization is Important

  • Makes complex data easy to understand
  • Helps in decision-making
  • Reveals hidden trends
  • Improves business performance

📊 Types of Charts

  • Bar Chart: Comparison
  • Line Chart: Trends over time
  • Pie Chart: Distribution
  • Scatter Plot: Relationships

⚙️ What is Power BI?

Power BI is a business intelligence tool used to create interactive dashboards and reports.

  • Drag & drop interface
  • Real-time data updates
  • Interactive charts

🧠 What Makes a Good Dashboard?

  • Clear and simple design
  • Relevant data only
  • Proper chart selection
  • Easy to understand

🌍 Real-World Example

Companies use dashboards to track sales, customer behavior, and performance. Managers make decisions based on these insights.

🛠 Practical Analyst Tips

  • Always choose the right chart
  • Keep dashboards clean
  • Focus on insights, not design
  • Tell a story with data
